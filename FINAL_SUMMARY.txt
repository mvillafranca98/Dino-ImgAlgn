🎯 TUNING PARAMETERS:

--box-threshold (default: 0.35)
  • Lower (0.2-0.3) = more detections
  • Higher (0.4-0.5) = fewer false positives

--text-threshold (default: 0.25)
  • Lower (0.15-0.2) = flexible matching
  • Higher (0.3-0.35) = strict matching
